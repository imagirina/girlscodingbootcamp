Is this Bootcamp for you?

Who are we?

We are a community of women who want to change a career path, jump into a new field or stay fit by preparing themselves for technical interviews.

We are learning together and supporting each other for success at interviews and careers in tech.

What will you learn?

We will start by solving many problems that's why initial programming skills are a must.

We'll try to cover the most popular interview topics with attention to patterns and your ability to navigate between them.

By the end of the bootcamp, we'll show how to solve 50-100 LeetCode problems, discuss your solutions in terms of time and space complexity, edge cases, and testing.

Preparing for an interview is a long and hard process, we'll simplify certain steps for you, but your efforts and ability to dedicate time will be also crucial.

What are the prerequisites?

  • To be more or less confident programming in Python or any other language
  • To have knowledge beyond arrays and loops
  • To be familiar with Data Structures (there will be a test)
  • To solve easy level problems (there will be a test)
  • To have ability and desire to spend 5-15 hours a week (solving problems, reading solutions and learning Python / another language, supporting other participants)

Our Goals

Primary goals: learn solving problems to excel at Tech Interviews and build a supportive community.

Secondary goals: support women in job search for a position in Tech and advancement of their careers.

What if I need help?

Being stuck at any level is a natural thing during the learning process. That's why we are together. Ways to get support:

  • Self-learning teams are teams of 4-6 women that meet up often and will become your source of immediate support.
  • Slack channels on any topic from resume reviewing to asking about a solution for a specific LeetCode problem.
  • Contact organizers directly with any questions. We'll try to answer with our experience or find the best contact for you.
